About

Apartments D & N are located in Vis, 600 meters from the city beach Prirovo, and have air conditioning, free WiFi and access to the terrace. All units include tiled floors, a seating area with a flat-screen cable TV, a fully equipped kitchen with a dining area, and a private bathroom. Guests can use the fridge, oven, hob and electric kettle. The facility is located near popular attractions, such as Zmorac, Vagan beaches and Fort George. The details that you will encounter in our house offer you countless opportunities for beautiful selfies that will bring a smile back to your face even when you leave our island, but we also hope to make you want to come back to us again. As hosts, we are at your disposal for everything you need, and we will be extremely pleased if we can satisfy your every request! THE HOSTS ARE VACCINATED WITH THE PFIZER VACCINE! :) The facility is located in the immediate vicinity of all amenities: tavern-pizzeria, shop, clinic, currency exchange, fast food, park, ferry, outdoor gym near the beach and the city beach itself.
